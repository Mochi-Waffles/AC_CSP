ZION NATIONAL PARK

Have you ever been hopelessly searching for a place to go for a vacation? Why not give Utah a chance? More specifically, Zion National Parks. Within Zion there are many places to explore and admire. Some of the most famous being angels landing; a beautiful rock formation, The Narrows; a shallow river to hike in with large walls of rock around you, and The vergin River; a scenic river you can go tubing in. Still not interested? Here's some cool facts that could help you change your mind.

  • Zion is home to many different animals, 78 species of mammals and more.
  • All of the formations in Zion were formed 250 million years ago.
  • There's a lot of interesting history for the area, especially the name, so check that out.
  • Zion has an endangered species, the California Condor, living peacefully and safely within the area.
